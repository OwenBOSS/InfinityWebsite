PIM's Maze

Welcome!

This project generates a maze using pim's algorithm. Soon you will be able to click through the maze to try to solve it! You stat at the green square and your goal is the red square.

Pim's Algorithm

The algorithm generates a list of potential next spots to expand the maze. We then select one at random. This process repeats until the play area is filled.

How to play

Try to get through the maze without going diagonally, only one the gray squares. Start at green and end at red.
