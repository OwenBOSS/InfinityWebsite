PIM's Maze

Welcome!

This project generates a maze using pim's algorithm. Soon you will be able to click through the maze to try to solve it! You stat at the green square and your goal is the red square.

Pim's Algorithm

The algorithm generates a list of potential next spots to expand the maze. We then select one at random. This process repeats until the play area is filled.

How to play

Goal: to reach the end of the maze.
Start: the green square.
End: the red square.

  1. Wait for the maze to finish generating
  2. Select one of the yellow squares by left clicking
  3. Repeat untill you reach the end of the maze!

Beware! If you make a wrong move you will have to restart with a new maze! For now just refresh the page ("I'm stuck" button comming soon).

If you make it to the end the play area will turn green. If you wish to play again simply refresh the page ("Play again" button comming soon!).
